The Hub Welcomes You

If you are seeking an alliance with a private community,
you have come to the right place.
The Hub was never meant to be owned by just me, but all of you.
You will all be able to develop this site as I have and have equal ownership.

It's simple, I want to bring together a pagan community who can trust and depend on one another.
You will be required to send me an email with a description of yourself,
the pagan religion you serve and why you feel this community is right for you.
Only your full name is required, (as far as personal information goes) If you chose to use an alias,
you may certainly do so with your profile.
I will get back to you as quickly as possible and if you are accepted,
a login and password will be provided to you.
If accepted, we'll personally develop your profile page together to fit your needs,
unless you want the template we use.
It is our most sacred policy to keep your privacy protected. Pagan's protect one another at all costs.
